Why NONETPAY

Built for the Real World

Designed for areas with poor or no connectivity — rural markets, underground venues, flight mode.

📵
100% Offline Payments
Generate and receive signed vouchers without any internet. Sync later when connectivity returns.
🔐
Cryptographic Security
Every voucher is signed with ECDSA secp256k1 — the same cryptography used in Bitcoin. Impossible to forge.
📱
QR Code Payments
Scan merchant QR codes to pay instantly. Works even with airplane mode on both devices.
🔄
Auto Sync
Transactions queue locally and sync automatically the moment internet is restored — no manual action needed.
🚫
No Double Spend
Each voucher has a unique ID. The server rejects duplicates — even presented multiple times offline before syncing.
🤖

Built-in AI analyzes your spending patterns and gives smart financial suggestions in natural language.
Flow

How It Works

A simple, three-step process that works entirely offline.

1
Add Balance Online
Top up your wallet using Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking). Balance is stored securely on server and cached locally.
2
Pay Offline via QR
Tap Pay → scan merchant QR → app generates a signed voucher deducting balance locally. No internet needed at any step.
3
Merchant Accepts & Syncs
Merchant scans your voucher QR. When either party goes online, the voucher syncs and merchant is credited instantly.
4
Server Verifies & Settles
Backend verifies the ECDSA signature, checks for duplicates, and settles the transaction — guaranteeing integrity end-to-end.
